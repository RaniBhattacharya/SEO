1. What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
ANS:-
=>SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is a digital marketing strategy aimed at improving a website's visibility in search engine results. It involves optimizing content and technical aspects to rank higher organically. SEO is crucial as it drives organic traffic, enhances online presence, and boosts website credibility, ultimately increasing business opportunities.






3.What are the benefits of using meta tags?
ANS:-
=>Meta tags offer several benefits for website optimization and user experience:

1. Improved Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Meta tags, such as meta titles and descriptions, help search engines understand the content of a web page, which can lead to higher rankings in search results.

2. Increased Click-Through Rates (CTR): Well-crafted meta descriptions can entice users to click on your link, leading to higher CTR and more organic traffic.

3. Enhanced User Experience: Meta tags provide a brief preview of the content, helping users quickly determine if a webpage is relevant to their needs.

4. Better Social Sharing: Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags can improve how your content appears when shared on social media, making it more appealing and engaging.

5. Structured Data: Some meta tags, like schema.org markup, can provide search engines with structured data, leading to rich snippets in search results, which can improve visibility and trustworthiness.

Overall, meta tags play a crucial role in optimizing web pages for both search engines and users, resulting in better visibility, more clicks, and a positive user experience.



5.Define Favicon and give an illustrative example.
ANS:-
=>A favicon, short for "favorite icon," is a small, typically 16x16-pixel image or icon displayed in a web browser's address bar or next to the site's name in bookmarks. It helps identify and brand a website. Here's an illustrative example:

Imagine you're visiting a website about a music store. In the browser tab or bookmark list, you might see a tiny guitar icon (the favicon) next to the site's name. This guitar icon is the favicon, representing the website and making it easily recognizable to users.
